I am a Computer Information Systems student at Arizona State University, Class of 2027. This website is my first full portfolio-style site, so I wanted the content to sound like me and match where I actually am as a student.

Why I Built This Site

I built this portfolio for CIS 300, but I also wanted it to be something I could keep using after the class. Instead of making a site that only checks boxes, I tried to create a clean starting point that can grow as I finish more projects and get more experience.

I kept the design simple on purpose. The blue theme, cards, and clear sections make the site feel professional without making it look like I copied a giant template.
